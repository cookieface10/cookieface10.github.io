A Wolf's Vengeance

Target Platforms:Pc

Development and Languages:Unity, C#

Game Genre:3D, Platformer, Challenging

Play Time:1-3 hours

Dec 3rd 2022

Description:

A Wolf's Vengeance is a 3D, Adventure Platformer with open zone level design that allows the player to decide what order to play levels in.

You play as a wolf who needs to save the world after an alien attack. Your job is to go through rifts and free the human soles.

Intentions:

A Wolf's Vengence was built as a long term project with the intention of using it to learn more about the elements needed in most games (e.g., UI, post processing, partical effects, save systems, 3D modeling, story telling, playing mp4 files and terrain tools). Throughout the development process there were a lot of incremental edits made to improve the quality of the game.

Pill Parkour

Target Platforms:Pc, Web

Development and Languages:Unity, C#

Game Genre:2.5D, Platformer

Play Time:20-30 Minutes

Oct 9th 2021

Description:

Pill Parkour is a 2.5D Platformer where you go through the 6 zones(5 normal areas + 1 secret area).

you play as a basic unity capsule. that jumps from block to block to get to the end.

Intentions:

This was my first game in unity and was used to learn how the engine and C# works.

The idea was that I would make 5 areas for the game, all with unique gameplay mechanics to keep the game interesting as you went through the levels.

There are still some problems with the game, like how you can get stuck on the corner of a block because of how the ground check works.
